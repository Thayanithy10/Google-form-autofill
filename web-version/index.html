Form
AutoFill

Drag the button to your bookmarks bar. Click it once on any form — it auto-fills every page till submission.

Step 1 — drag to bookmarks bar do once

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar
(Press Ctrl+Shift+B to show the bar first)

⚡ AutoFill Form
✅ Click this bookmark once when you open a form
✅ It watches ALL pages automatically — no clicking per page
✅ Pauses on elective pages so you can pick your subject
✅ Shows a confirm popup before final submit
Step 2 — every time you fill a form every time
1
Open the Google Form
Open the form link from your teacher as normal.
2
Click ⚡ AutoFill Form in bookmarks bar
An orange badge appears at the bottom-right. Script is now watching the entire form.
3
Fill page 1 → click Next
Enter name, roll number manually. Click Next — script takes over from page 2.
4
Every page fills automatically
No more clicking per page. On elective pages a popup asks you to pick your subject, then continues automatically.
For the admin — host on GitHub Pages
1
Go to github.com → create a new repo named form-autofill
2
Upload autofill.js and rename this file to index.html and upload both
3
Go to Settings → Pages → Branch: main → Save
4
Update the SCRIPT_URL in this file to https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/form-autofill/autofill.js
5
Share https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/form-autofill with all students via WhatsApp
